<p><p>The premature infant incubator market comprises two primary types: transport infant incubators and normal infant incubators. Transport incubators are designed for safe and effective movement of premature infants between healthcare facilities, ensuring temperature control and stability during transit. Normal infant incubators, on the other hand, provide a controlled environment for premature infants within hospital settings, maintaining optimal humidity and temperature levels to support their development. Both types are crucial for enhancing neonatal care and improving survival rates for vulnerable infants.</p></p>
