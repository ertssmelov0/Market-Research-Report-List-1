<p><p>Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM) is a synthetic rubber known for its excellent weather resistance, heat stability, and flexibility. The EPDM market can be segmented based on manufacturing processes: solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. </p><p>Solution polymerization involves dissolving monomers in a solvent to create a homogeneous mixture, resulting in a high-quality, consistent product ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Conversely, suspension polymerization creates EPDM through a dispersed environment, yielding a coarser texture, suitable for rubber roofing and insulation. Each method offers unique benefits tailored to specific applications.</p></p>
