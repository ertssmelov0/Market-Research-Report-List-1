<p><p>A Lime Rotary Kiln is a highly efficient industrial furnace used for the production of lime from limestone through a process of calcination. This equipment operates at elevated temperatures and facilitates the chemical transformation of calcium carbonate into calcium oxide, commonly known as quicklime. The lime produced is essential in various applications, including metallurgy, construction, agriculture, and environmental management.</p><p>The Lime Rotary Kiln Market is witnessing significant growth due to increasing demand for lime in various end-user industries, such as steel manufacturing, water treatment, and soil stabilization.
