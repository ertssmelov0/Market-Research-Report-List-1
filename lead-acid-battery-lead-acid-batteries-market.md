<p><ul><li>VRLA Battery</li><li>Flooded Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lead-acid battery market is primarily divided into two types: VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid) batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed and require little maintenance, making them ideal for applications like renewable energy storage and UPS systems. Conversely, flooded batteries contain a liquid electrolyte and require regular maintenance, providing high power output and durability, suitable for automotive and industrial uses. Both types play crucial roles in various sectors, balancing performance and maintenance needs in energy storage solutions.</p></p>
