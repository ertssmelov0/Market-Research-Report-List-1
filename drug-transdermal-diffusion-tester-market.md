<p><p>The Drug Transdermal Diffusion Tester market is segmented into vertical and horizontal types based on the orientation of the diffusion chamber. Vertical testers facilitate the upward diffusion of drugs, suitable for studying substances reacting to gravity and ensuring uniform distribution. In contrast, horizontal testers allow for lateral diffusion, making them ideal for formulations that depend on surface area and film thickness. Both types serve essential roles in evaluating drug delivery systems' efficacy across various transdermal applications.</p></p>
