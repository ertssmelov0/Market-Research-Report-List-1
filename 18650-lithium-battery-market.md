<p><p>The 18650 lithium battery market comprises several types, each with distinct characteristics. The LiCoO2 (Lithium Cobalt Oxide) battery offers high energy density and is often used in portable electronics. NMC (Nickel Manganese Cobalt) and NCA (Nickel Cobalt Aluminum) batteries provide a balance of energy density, longevity, and thermal stability, making them suitable for electric vehicles. LiFePO4 (Lithium Iron Phosphate) batteries focus on safety and longer cycle life, ideal for energy storage. Other battery types include specialized chemistries for niche applications.</p></p>
