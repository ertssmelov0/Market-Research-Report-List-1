<p><strong>The Underfloor Heating and Cooling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric System</li><li>Hydronic System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underfloor heating and cooling systems consist of two main types: electric and hydronic systems. Electric systems use electrically heated cables or mats installed beneath the floor, offering quick installation and precise temperature control. Conversely, hydronic systems circulate heated or cooled water through pipes under the flooring, providing consistent warmth and energy efficiency, especially for larger areas. Both systems enhance comfort while optimizing energy use, making them popular choices in residential and commercial environments.</p></p>
