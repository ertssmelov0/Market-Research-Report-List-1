<p><p>The audiology devices market comprises several key types, including hearing aids, cochlear implants, diagnostic devices, and bone anchored hearing aids (BAHA). Hearing aids amplify sound for individuals with hearing loss, while cochlear implants provide direct stimulation to the auditory nerve for severe cases. Diagnostic devices assess hearing capabilities, aiding in accurate diagnosis and treatment planning. BAHA devices attach to the skull, transmitting sound vibrations directly to the inner ear, offering an alternative for those with conductive hearing loss.</p></p>
