<p><p>The Ultrasonic Show-B Diagnostic Equipment market is categorized into two main types: Continuous Wave Ultrasound Equipment and Pulsed Wave Ultrasound Equipment. Continuous Wave Ultrasound operates continuously, providing real-time imaging and better penetration, making it ideal for applications requiring steady signals. In contrast, Pulsed Wave Ultrasound sends short bursts of sound waves, allowing for precise depth measurement and improved resolution in imaging. Both types are vital in various medical diagnostics, offering unique advantages based on clinical needs.</p></p>
