<p><p>Infrared radiant heaters are categorized into high-intensity and low-intensity types based on their heating mechanisms and applications. High-intensity infrared heaters emit concentrated heat, making them ideal for large spaces and industrial settings, where rapid warmth is required. In contrast, low-intensity infrared heaters provide more uniform, gentle warmth suited for smaller areas or residential use, such as in garages or workshops. Both types offer energy efficiency, but their selection depends on the specific heating needs of the environment.</p></p>
