<p><p>Carbon Nanotubes (CNTs) are cylindrical nanostructures with exceptional mechanical, electrical, and thermal properties. The market is primarily segmented into two types: Single-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (SWCNTs) and Multi-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (MWCNTs). SWCNTs consist of a single layer of carbon atoms, offering unique electrical characteristics, making them ideal for applications in electronics and nanocomposites. MWCNTs, comprising multiple concentric layers, provide enhanced mechanical strength and are widely used in various industries, including aerospace, energy storage, and materials science.</p></p>
