<p><p>The synthetic zeolite adsorbents market is divided into two primary types: regenerative and non-regenerative zeolite adsorbents. In contrast, non-regenerative zeolite adsorbents are typically used once and then discarded, often serving specific, short-term purposes. Both types are essential in industries such as environmental remediation, petrochemicals, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
