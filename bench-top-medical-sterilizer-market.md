<p><p>The benchtop medical sterilizer market comprises various types, including steam sterilizers, plasma sterilizers, and others. Steam sterilizers use high-pressure steam to eliminate microorganisms, making them suitable for heat and moisture-resistant medical instruments. Plasma sterilizers utilize low-temperature hydrogen peroxide plasma for sterilization, ideal for heat-sensitive items. Additionally, "others" encompass alternative sterilization methods such as dry heat or ethylene oxide, catering to diverse needs in healthcare settings. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficacy of medical equipment.</p></p>
