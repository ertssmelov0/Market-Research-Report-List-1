<p><p>The Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) market is categorized based on the comonomer used in its production, primarily C4, C6, and C8 types. C4-LLDPE utilizes butene as a comonomer, offering flexibility and impact resistance, suitable for packaging and agricultural films. C6-LLDPE, made with hexene, provides enhanced clarity and strength, commonly used in blow molding and multi-layer films. C8-LLDPE, incorporating octene, delivers even greater elasticity and sealing properties, making it ideal for premium packaging applications and durable goods.</p></p>
