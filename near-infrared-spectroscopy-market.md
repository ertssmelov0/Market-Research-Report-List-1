<p><p>Near Infrared Spectroscopy (NIRS) market is segmented into three main types: Single Beam, Double Beam, and Other methods. </p><p>Single Beam NIRS utilizes one beam of light for measurement, often resulting in simpler and more affordable systems. Double Beam NIRS employs two beams—one for the sample and one for a reference—enhancing measurement accuracy by compensating for fluctuations in light source intensity. Other methods may include advanced technologies like fiber optic or imaging systems, catering to specialized applications across various industries like pharmaceuticals, food, and agriculture.</p></p>
