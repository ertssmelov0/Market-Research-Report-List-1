<p><strong>The Defibrillator Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator</li><li>External Cardioverter Defibrillator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Defibrillator devices are critical in managing life-threatening cardiac arrhythmias. The market comprises two primary types: Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) and External Cardioverter Defibrillators (ECDs). ICDs are surgically implanted devices that continuously monitor heart rhythm and deliver shocks when abnormal rhythms are detected, providing long-term treatment for at-risk patients. In contrast, ECDs are portable devices used in emergency situations to deliver shocks externally, aiding first responders in treating sudden cardiac arrest effectively and rapidly in various settings.</p></p>
