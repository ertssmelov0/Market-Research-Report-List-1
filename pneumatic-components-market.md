<p><ul><li>Actuators</li><li>Control Element</li><li>Air Treatment Components</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic components market comprises three main types: actuators, control elements, and air treatment components. Actuators convert pneumatic energy into mechanical motion, facilitating movement in machines and processes. Control elements, such as valves and sensors, regulate airflow and pressure, ensuring precise operation and automation within systems. Air treatment components, including filters, lubricators, and dryers, maintain the quality of compressed air, protecting equipment from contaminants and enhancing performance. Together, these components support various industries by improving efficiency and reliability in pneumatic systems.</p></p>
