<p><p>The artificial cornea and corneal implant market can be categorized into two main types: human cornea and artificial cornea. Human corneas are harvested from donors for transplantation, addressing conditions like keratoconus and corneal scarring. In contrast, artificial corneas, also known as keratoprostheses, are synthetic devices designed for patients who cannot benefit from traditional transplants due to factors like severe ocular surface disease. Both types aim to restore vision and improve quality of life for individuals with corneal diseases.</p></p>
