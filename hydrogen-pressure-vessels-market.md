<p><strong>The Hydrogen Pressure Vessels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Hydrogen pressure vessels are categorized into four types based on their construction and materials. Type I vessels are all-metal, featuring high strength but considerable weight. Type II vessels are metal-lined with a composite overwrap, reducing weight while maintaining pressure integrity. Type III vessels are fully composite with a metal liner, offering a lightweight solution and excellent performance. Type IV vessels utilize a polymer liner encased in a composite shell, providing the lightest option ideal for high-capacity storage applications.</p></p>
