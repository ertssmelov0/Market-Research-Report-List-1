<p><ul><li>Viscous</li><li>Inviscid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical eye patches market is segmented into viscous and inviscid types based on their adhesive properties and application methods. Viscous eye patches are designed with thicker, more adhesive materials that provide stronger attachment to the skin, ensuring stability and protection. In contrast, inviscid eye patches utilize lighter, less tacky materials, offering comfort and ease of application. Both types cater to different patient needs, with viscous patches often preferred for post-surgery use and inviscid patches selected for long-term wear and comfort.</p></p>
