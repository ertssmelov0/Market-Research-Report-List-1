<p><p>The water filters and purification market encompasses various technologies, notably Reverse Osmosis (RO) and Ultrafiltration (UF) membranes. RO systems utilize a semi-permeable membrane to remove contaminants and impurities by applying pressure, effectively producing purified water. Ultrafiltration, on the other hand, employs a membrane with larger pores to filter out suspended solids, bacteria, and larger viruses while retaining essential minerals. Both methods cater to residential, commercial, and industrial needs, promoting access to safe drinking water and enhancing water quality globally.</p></p>
