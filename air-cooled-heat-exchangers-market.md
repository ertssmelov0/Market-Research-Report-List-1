<p><p>Air-Cooled Heat Exchangers (ACHEs) are devices used to transfer heat between two fluids without the need for a cooling tower or water supply. These exchangers utilize air as the cooling medium, making them particularly advantageous in areas where water is scarce or regulations limit water usage. ACHEs find extensive applications in industries such as oil and gas, power generation, petrochemical, and food processing.</p><p>The market for Air-Cooled Heat Exchangers is experiencing robust growth, driven by a surge in industrial activities and the increasing need for energy efficiency.
