<p><strong>The Clutch Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push-Type Clutch Bearing</li><li>Pull-Type Clutch Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clutch bearings market is primarily divided into two types: push-type and pull-type clutch bearings. Push-type clutch bearings are designed to engage the clutch by applying force in a forward direction, making them suitable for vehicles that require consistent engagement pressure. Conversely, pull-type clutch bearings operate by retracting or pulling back the clutch, allowing for smoother disengagement and better control in certain applications. Both types play critical roles in automotive performance and efficiency, catering to different vehicle requirements.</p></p>
