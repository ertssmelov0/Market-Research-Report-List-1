<p><p>The smart dash camera market is divided into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel cameras feature one lens and primarily capture the forward view, making them cost-effective and suitable for basic monitoring. In contrast, multi-channel cameras incorporate multiple lenses, often providing 360-degree coverage, including rear and side views. This enhanced functionality allows for comprehensive surveillance, catering to users who require detailed evidence of incidents from various angles. As technology advances, both types serve distinct user needs in vehicle safety and security.</p></p>
