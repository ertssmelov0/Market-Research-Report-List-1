<p><strong>The Industrial X-ray Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screen Type Films</li><li>Non-Screen Type Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial X-ray film market is categorized into two primary types: screen type films and non-screen type films. Screen type films are designed to be used with fluorescent screens, which enhance image quality and reduce radiation exposure by converting X-rays into visible light. In contrast, non-screen type films are more sensitive to X-rays directly, producing high-resolution images without the need for screens. Each type serves specific industrial applications, including inspections and quality control in manufacturing and construction sectors.</p></p>
