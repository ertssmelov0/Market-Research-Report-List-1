<p><p>The electric and hybrid aircraft propulsion system market encompasses two key types: hybrid powertrains and all-electric powertrains. Hybrid powertrains combine traditional combustion engines with electric motors, allowing for reduced emissions and increased fuel efficiency while maintaining range. In contrast, all-electric powertrains utilize solely electric motors powered by batteries, offering zero emissions during flight but limited range and charge times. Both systems aim to enhance sustainability in aviation while addressing performance, operational efficiency, and regulatory requirements for greener technologies.</p></p>
