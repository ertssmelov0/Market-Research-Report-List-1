<p><p>The graphite packing market encompasses various types of products, each suited for specific applications. Dense crystalline graphite packing features tightly arranged particles, providing excellent thermal and chemical resistance. Flake graphite packing consists of larger, flat particles, offering high lubrication and conductivity. Cryptocrystalline graphite packing is composed of extremely fine particles, enhancing flexibility and sealing capabilities. These distinct types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and chemical processing, addressing varying requirements for durability, performance, and efficiency in sealing applications.</p></p>
