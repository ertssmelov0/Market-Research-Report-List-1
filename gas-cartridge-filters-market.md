<p><strong>The Gas Cartridge Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Filters</li><li>Depth Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas cartridge filters market is divided into two main types: surface filters and depth filters. Surface filters capture particles on their outer layer, providing efficient filtration for larger contaminants while allowing cleaner gases to pass through. In contrast, depth filters trap particles within their porous matrix, offering enhanced filtration for smaller particles and a greater dirt-holding capacity. Both types play essential roles in various applications, including industrial processes and environmental management, ensuring air quality and compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
