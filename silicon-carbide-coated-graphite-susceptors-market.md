<p><p>Silicon carbide coated graphite susceptors are essential components in various thermal processing applications, particularly in semiconductor manufacturing. The market offers distinct types, including pancake and barrel susceptors. Pancake susceptors are flat, disc-shaped, allowing for uniform heat distribution and efficient energy transfer in processes like chemical vapor deposition. Barrel susceptors, on the other hand, are cylindrical and are designed for vertical applications, providing effective thermal management in more complex geometries and processes, enhancing overall efficiency and performance in manufacturing environments.</p></p>
