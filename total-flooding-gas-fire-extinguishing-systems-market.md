<p><p>Total flooding gas fire extinguishing systems are categorized into three main types: Carbon Dioxide Fire Suppression Systems, which use CO2 to displace oxygen and smother fires; Chemical Gas Fire Suppression Systems that deploy clean agents to interrupt the combustion process; and Inert Gas Fire Suppression Systems that utilize gases like nitrogen and argon to reduce oxygen levels in the protected area. Each type is designed to effectively control or extinguish fires in enclosed spaces without causing damage to sensitive equipment.</p></p>
