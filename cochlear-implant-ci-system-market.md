<p><p>The cochlear implant (CI) system market is primarily segmented into unilateral and binaural cochlear implants. Unilateral cochlear implants are designed for individuals with hearing loss in one ear, providing significant benefits in speech perception and environmental sound awareness. In contrast, binaural cochlear implants involve the implantation of devices in both ears, enhancing spatial hearing, sound localization, and overall auditory experience. This dual approach often leads to improved listening abilities in noisy environments and greater overall satisfaction for users.</p></p>
