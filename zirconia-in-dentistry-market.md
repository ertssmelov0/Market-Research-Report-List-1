<p><ul><li>Low Transparency</li><li>Medium Transparency</li><li>High Transparency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Zirconia in dentistry is classified into three market types based on transparency: low, medium, and high transparency. Low transparency zirconia offers excellent strength and is often used for posterior restorations where aesthetics are less critical. Medium transparency provides a balance between strength and aesthetics, making it suitable for both anterior and posterior applications. High transparency zirconia is designed to mimic natural tooth appearance closely, ideal for anterior restorations, offering superior aesthetics while still maintaining adequate strength for functional use.</p></p>
