<p><p>The Electrotherapy Pain Relief System market includes various modalities, primarily Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) and Microcurrent Nerve Stimulation (MENS). TENS uses low-voltage electrical currents to stimulate nerves, effectively alleviating pain by blocking pain signals and promoting the release of endorphins. MENS, on the other hand, delivers microcurrents to tissues, enhancing cellular repair and reducing inflammation. Both systems are popular for managing chronic pain and muscle tension, offering non-invasive alternatives to medication and surgery.</p></p>
