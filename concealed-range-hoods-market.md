<p><ul><li>Axial Flow</li><li>Centrifugal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concealed range hoods market is segmented into axial flow and centrifugal types. Axial flow hoods use fans to draw air directly through the blades, providing efficient ventilation at lower noise levels, making them suitable for residential settings. In contrast, centrifugal hoods utilize a more complex design to increase air pressure, effectively handling high volumes of smoke and odors, making them ideal for commercial kitchens. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences and kitchen requirements, enhancing air quality and cooking experiences.</p></p>
