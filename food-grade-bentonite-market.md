<p><p>The food-grade bentonite market comprises primarily two types: sodium bentonite and calcium bentonite. Sodium bentonite is known for its high swelling capacity and is often used in food processing as a clarifying agent and for its anti-caking properties. Calcium bentonite, with its lower swelling ability, is commonly utilized in various food applications for its absorbent qualities and nutritional benefits. Both types play essential roles in enhancing food quality, safety, and shelf life within the food industry.</p></p>
